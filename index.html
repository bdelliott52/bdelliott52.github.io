About

At my core I am an entertainer. As a double graduate from the University of Florida's College of Journalism and Communications, my talents lie in producing, directing, editing, and sharing stories. I feel that all good stories have one universal common element that makes them entertaining: an interesting character. Finding that character - or creating them from scratch - is what makes me love what I do.
