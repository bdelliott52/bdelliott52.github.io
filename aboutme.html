About

As a videographer and media specialist my areas of expertise are filmmaking, editing, writing, and research. I have experience in creative film and commercial production, live sports production, and web design.

I’m a double graduate of the University of Florida's Journalism and Communications school with my MA in Mass Communication (Digital Media) and BS in Telecommunication (Video Production).

I’ve filmed sporting events for UF’s Gatorvision and also worked with live production crews for ESPN. Independently I produce commercial projects for businesses. If you would like to work with me, you can reach me by using the contact information at the bottom of the page.

In my opinion the greatest stories have one universal common element that makes them entertaining: an interesting character. Finding that character - or creating them from scratch - is what makes me love what I do.
